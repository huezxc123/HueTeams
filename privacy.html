Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

📋 Cuckoo is committed to protecting the privacy of employers, employees, and all users of our platform. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it — in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Company name, admin email address, password (hashed)
  • Employee records: Names, email addresses, employee IDs, department, role, and monthly salary
  • Attendance data: Clock-in/out timestamps, IP addresses, late minutes
  • Leave requests: Leave type, dates, reason, and approval status
  • Payroll data: Computed gross pay, deductions, and net pay per period
  • Usage data: Pages visited, actions performed (audit logs), and browser/device information

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and operate the Cuckoo platform
  • Compute payroll, attendance, and HR reports
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
  • Maintain security through audit logs and access controls
  • Improve and develop the Service

We do not sell your data to third parties, nor do we use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Realtime Database), hosted in the Asia-Southeast region. Firebase provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS). Access is controlled through Firebase Authentication and database security rules.

We implement role-based access controls within the application to ensure employees can only access their own data, and only administrators can access company-wide records.

4. IP Address Collection

When an employee clocks in, we record their public IP address for audit and security purposes. This helps employers detect unusual access patterns. IP data is stored alongside attendance records and is visible only to company administrators.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account termination, you may request export of your data. We will delete company data from our servers within 90 days of account termination.

6. Your Rights Under RA 10173

As a data subject (employee or employer), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data held by us
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to certain types of processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, contact your company administrator or reach us at privacy@cuckoo.com.

7. Employer Responsibilities

As an employer using Cuckoo, you act as a personal information controller under RA 10173. You are responsible for:

  • Obtaining valid consent from employees before enrolling them in the system
  • Informing employees about how their data will be used
  • Ensuring employee data is accurate and up to date
  • Complying with all applicable data privacy regulations

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and database hosting
  • ipapi.co — IP geolocation at clock-in (optional; only for audit purposes)
  • Google Fonts / Font Awesome — UI assets (no personal data shared)

9. Cookies

Cuckoo uses Firebase Authentication session cookies to keep you logged in. These are essential cookies and cannot be disabled while using the Service. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
